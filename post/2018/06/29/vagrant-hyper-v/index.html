Vagrant Hyper V

29/06/2018

vagrant box add hashicorp/precise64 –provider hyperv

vagrant up –provider hyperv

After running vagrant init, modify your vagrant file with the following:

Vagrant.configure(2) do |config|
config.vm.box = “hashicorp/precise64” config.vm.provider “hyperv” config.vm.network “public_network” end
